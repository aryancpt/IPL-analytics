# 3.1. Season Overview & Key KPIs - The dashboard presents high-level season metrics such as:
- Total teams, matches, and venues
- Aggregate batting indicators like total fours and sixes
- Number of centuries and half-centuries
These KPIs help stakeholders quickly assess the scale, competitiveness, and entertainment value of the season.
# 3.2. Team Performance Analysis - Team performance is evaluated using
- Points table showing matches played, wins, losses, no-results, and total points
- Champion and runner-up highlights for quick identification of top-performing teams
This allows businesses, analysts, and fans to understand team consistency, qualification trends, and season dominance.
# 3.3. Player Performance Insights - Individual excellence is captured through
- Orange Cap statistics to measure top run-scorers
-	Purple Cap statistics to evaluate leading wicket-takers
These KPIs help in identifying standout performers and emerging talent during the season.
# 3.4. Batting Impact Metrics (4s & 6s Analysis) - The dashboard highlights:
-	Players with the most fours and sixes
-	Aggression and boundary-hitting capability of batsmen
This provides insights into playing styles, match-impact players, and fan-engagement drivers.
